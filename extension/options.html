Speaker Diarization (Who's Speaking?)

üéØ Setup Guide

Speaker diarization identifies WHO is speaking in the audio. Follow these steps to enable it:

  1. Create Account: Sign up free at huggingface.co/join
  2. Accept License #1: Go to pyannote/segmentation-3.0 and click "Agree and access repository"
  3. Accept License #2: Go to pyannote/speaker-diarization-3.1 and click "Agree and access repository"
  4. Create Token: Go to Settings ‚Üí Access Tokens ‚Üí "New token" ‚Üí Select "Read" ‚Üí Copy token
  5. Paste Below: Enter your token starting with hf_

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: You MUST accept BOTH model licenses (steps 2 & 3) or diarization will fail!

Your token is stored locally and only sent to Hugging Face for model access.

üîß Troubleshooting

  • "Access denied" ‚Üí Accept both model licenses (steps 2 & 3 above)
  • "Token invalid" ‚Üí Create a new token at huggingface.co/settings/tokens
  • "No speakers detected" ‚Üí Audio may be too short or single speaker
  • "Out of memory" ‚Üí Use a smaller Whisper model (tiny or base)
